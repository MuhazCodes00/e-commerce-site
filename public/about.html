Who We Are

Premium Materials, Trusted Since 2009

M.O.B Eki Ventures started as a small cement distributor in Ikeja, Lagos. Over 15 years, we've grown into one of Nigeria's most comprehensive building materials suppliers, serving over 3,400 active clients nationwide.

We partner directly with leading manufacturers including Dangote, BUA, Dulux, and other certified brands to ensure every product meets the highest quality standards.

Whether you're building a family home, a commercial complex, or an industrial facility, our team is ready to source the right materials at competitive prices.
